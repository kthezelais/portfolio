Help birdy to avoid obstacles!

Embody Birdy, a drugged bird who's trying to escape his real world responsibility and tries to stay high for the rest of his life. For this, help Birdy to avoid obstacles on his path by avoiding green tubes facing him. Each avoided obstacles increase the score by +1. Try to get the higest score as possible!
